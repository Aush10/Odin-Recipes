Stir Fried rice

stir-fry cooked to perfection. The combination of cooked rice, savory sauces, and an array of colorful vegetables and proteins wok-tossed together creates an irresistible medley of flavors and textures. With each bite, you'll be transported to a culinary paradise, where the harmonious b end of ingredients leaves you craving for more.

Ingredients

  • chopped carrots 2/3 cup
  • Green Peas1/2 cup
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Eggs2
  • Cooked white rice3 cups
  • Soy sauce 1 tbs.
  • Sesame oil 2 tsp.

recipe

  1. gather the ingredients
  2. Place carrots in a small saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in peas, then immediately drain in a colander.
  3. cut the sprouts length-wise
  4. Heat a wok over high heat. Pour in vegetable oil, then stir in carrots, peas, and garlic; cook for about 30 seconds. Add eggs; stir quickly to scramble eggs with vegetables.
  5. Stir in cooked rice. Add soy sauce and toss rice to coat. Drizzle with sesame oil and toss again.
  6. Serve hot!
