Brussels Sprouts

There are a lot of ways to enjoy Brussels sprouts, but objectively the best way is to roast them in butter and oil until they turn crispy and golden brown.
The roasting process brings out their natural sweetness while creating a delightful contrast with their tender interior, resulting in a mouthwatering dish
that will convert the snootiest critic into a sprout devout.

Ingredients

  • Brussels Sprouts 1 lbs
  • Onions1/2 pound , no stems or roots
  • Bacon crumbles 2 to 3 strips
  • Butter 5 tablespoons
  • Olive Oil1/4 cup
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon wedge

recipe

  1. pre heat the oven to 250o
  2. melt the butter in the microwave and pour it int a large bowl
  3. cut the sprouts length-wise
  4. mix en the uncooked sprouts and your choice of seasoning Pepper if nothing else
  5. set the onions and bacon crumbles on a well oiled baking sheet and pour the oil over the tray allowing it to collect in the tray
  6. bake the bake the onions and bacon for about 12 minutes then add in the sprouts
  7. bake the sprouts for 17 minutes or until golden on the outside
