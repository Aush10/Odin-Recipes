Lasagna

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with a mouthwatering lasagna recipe. Layers of perfectly cooked pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and a generous amount of melted mozzarella come together to create a symphony of flavors. Baked to perfection, this hearty and delicious lasagna is guaranteed to satisfy your cravings and leave your taste buds begging for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Meat2 cups
  • Onions 1 lbs
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Tomato paste1
  • Lasagna noodles1 box
  • Cheese 1 tbs.
  • Egg 2 tsp.
  • Can of Chef Boyardee lasagnarama 1.

recipe

  1. put the can of chef Boyardee Lasagnarama in the microwave and heat as per the instructions on the can remember to remove the tin lid
  2. prepare your meat of choice by putting it into a plastic bag
  3. place that bag in the freezer
  4. prepare the onions all at once by placing them in the trash
  5. also throw out the remaining ingredients as well,but keep the cheese.
  6. by now your chef Boyardee Lasagnarama should be cool enough to eat, add cheese as desired
  7. Serve warm
